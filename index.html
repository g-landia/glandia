Welcome to the Land of Good!

G-Landia is a global community of people, businesses, celebrities and many others willing to improve in all spheres and share care with each other.

Everyone here is united by genuine values and standards which make our world better.

How does G-Landia work?

The first project is G-Olympic Games.
Just follow 3 easy steps and choose your:

1. New qualities and goals.

2. Motivators willing to support you.

3. Charity Funds helping our planet (optional).

By playing G-Olympics, you will get Gs (rewards) which you will use for different purposes.

Choosing Your Personal Goals.

“Conquer yourself and you will win thousands of battles”

We want you to succeed and always be inspired by your goals.

Be it giving up smoking, losing weight or traveling the world - it doesn't matter!

What matters is your will to change and be better!

Choosing Motivators that Care.

"When you want something, all the universe conspires in helping you to achieve it"

21-st century has brought new challenges to the world.
USD 1 trillion spent annually on advertising gives people not just another unneeded expense, but is also seen as a source of irritation and unsatisfaction. Many advertised goods and services are not appropriate for people, others are simply not affordable. Not being able to know people's real wishes and needs makes marketing inefficient and very expensive.
The future of any organization depends on how wealthy, succesfull, long-living and brand-loyal its clients are!

G-Landia handles your choices and sends them directly to your motivators.
To those offering better motivation to People, G-Landia will give highest rates and FREE VIP spots for their logos (links) to be seen globally.

Choosing Funds that Care (optional)

“The more you share, the more the World shares with you”

We invite you to support funds that you believe help the most. You then decide on a contribution that will be donated every time you break a promise or goal.
Why do G-People tackle problems such as: pollution, child mortality, diseases, starvation, animal extinction and many more?

- Because they care and are able to redirect some of their money saved by wise-spending.
