The Land of Good

G-Landia is a global community of people, businesses, celebrities, politicians and many others willing to improve in all spheres and share care with each other.

G-Landia is created for the sake of our Planet, Children and their Future. Everyone here is united by genuine values and standards which make our world better.

Join Today Learn More

Why is it important to join G-Landia?

  • To achieve excellent personal results quicker.

  • To help to get a prompt positive world-wide effect in different spheres (environment, children, health, education, safety and many others).

  • To make G-Landia even more attractive to motivators, so they can share their care with more of us.

How does G-Landia work?

The first project of G-Landia is called “G-Olympics”.

People around the world are invited to represent and lead their countries by:

1. Choosing and improving qualities and habits they consider most important for them.

2. Choosing motivators (both local and global companies) interested in helping general people to become more loyal, healthy, happy, wealthy, caring, sincere, etc, etc.

3. Supporting Charity Funds that care about our Planet and us the most.

As soon as you make 3 simple choices, the program will automatically create your personal page which will show your daily progress and how it contributes to "3G" (Good Global Goals) project.

It will also allow you to collect your “G”s (Rewards) that can be used by you for different purposes. Learn More +

Choosing Motivators that Care

21-st century has brought new challenges to global economies and businesses.

USD 1 trillion spent annually on advertising gives people not just another unneeded expense, but is also seen as a source of irritation and unsatisfaction. Many advertised goods and services are not appropriate for people, others are simply not affordable. Not being able to know peoples’ real wishes and needs makes marketing inefficient and very expensive.

All organizations in the world want their clients to be brand-loyal, rich, happy, long- living, successful, etc…

G-Landia collects your “MOTIVATION REQUESTs” and sends them directly to chosen companies, celebrities, politicians or even religious leaders.

We know that those organizations that offer more motivation and real care to their current or potential clients will always be highly respected and rewarded.

As for G-Landia, all MOTIVATORS participating and helping people to improve their lives are guaranteed free advertising (according to peoples key interests). Learn More +

Choosing Charity Funds that Care

“The more you share the more this World returns to you”

We ask all members to choose FUNDS that in their view help our Planet and People most.

You can then choose your donation (anything from USD 1 cent) that will be collected and transferred weekly to chosen FUNDS every time you break your promise / goal.

Why do G-People support global trends such as preventing diseases, child death, hunger, pollution, forest and animal disappearance and many more?

Because they care and are able to redirect some of their money saved by wise-spending. Learn More +
